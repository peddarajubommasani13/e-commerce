Shipping Information

Domestic Shipping (UK & EU)

We offer complimentary Standard Delivery on all orders above €150. For orders below €150, a flat rate of €10 applies. Delivery typically takes 3-5 business days. Express Delivery is available for €25, delivering in 1-2 business days.

International Shipping

We ship worldwide. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout depending on destination and weight. Delivery times vary from 5 to 10 business days. Custom duties and taxes are paid at checkout where applicable.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive an email containing your tracking number and a link to trace your shipment. You can also view tracking details directly inside your Account Dashboard under "My Orders".

Returns & Exchanges Policy

14-Day Return Window

We accept returns of unworn, unwashed, and undamaged items with all original tags attached within 14 days of receiving your order. Please note that sale items marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged.

How to Initiate a Return

To request a return, please log in to your account, navigate to "My Orders", select the order you wish to return, and click "Request Return". A prepaid shipping label will be generated for you. A return fee of €5 will be deducted from your refund amount.

Exchanges

If you need a different size or color, please initiate a return for the original item and place a new order online. This ensures the item is secured and shipped to you as quickly as possible.
